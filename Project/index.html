Welcome to Kingdom Multisport

Kingdom Multisport offers affordable triathlon and endurance sports coaching using customized training plans, experience, science, nutrition, and technology.

We understand what it's like to have many things to balance in life and build programs around the lives of our athletes rather than expecting life to conform to a training plan.

"I believe many people are capable of much more than they realize. What they lack is the confidence and having someone who believes in them and can help coach them to reach their dreams and goals."

Trevor Stultz, Coach and Owner of Kingdom Multisport
