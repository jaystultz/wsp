Our Coaching Philosophy

God has gifted us with the talents of being an endurance athletes and the ability to lift up others and help them be healthy physically. While walking down that path, we've learned and grown so much spiritually and know that as we lead others, they will as well. We don't believe "by the book" training programs work for most people. They are far too rigid and don't allow for the occurances in life which inevitably pop up. Our plans are designed to build training programs around life as it exists currently, with realistic, attainable goals. We're honest and up front, but also understanding.

Our training philosophy is simple: Structured, quality workouts are far more effective than high volume quantity workouts which often lead to over-training, injury, and mental burnout. We utilize customized periodization blocks, laid out in TrainingPeaks.com, with one-on-one interaction regularly with each athlete.

Many coaches out there currently have limitations to the interactions with each athlete. We allow for reasonable, unlimited access for our athletes so far as they are doing their part in completing the training as prescribed.

-Coach Trevor
